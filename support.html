Support

For help with Quran Within, report a problem or request assistance through the project's public GitHub Issues page.

Open support on GitHub

Please do not include sensitive information

GitHub Issues are public. Do not include private reflections, du'a text, passwords, recovery codes, payment details, or any other personal or sensitive information. Describe the issue generally, your app version, and your device/operating-system version instead.

Before reporting a problem

  • Update Quran Within to the latest available version.
  • Restart the app and try the action once more.
  • For reminders, check that notifications are allowed in your device settings.
  • For audio, check your network connection and your device's media volume.

Privacy questions

Please read the Privacy policy before contacting support. The app does not create user accounts or maintain a server copy of your private reflections and du'a entries, so support cannot recover deleted local data.
